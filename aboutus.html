About Us

Gitam BookStore is a student-to-student marketplace designed to make buying and selling textbooks easier and more affordable for students at Gitam University. Our platform bridges the gap between juniors and seniors, allowing them to exchange books at reasonable prices, thereby fostering a culture of sharing and sustainability on campus.

At Gitam BookStore, we understand the financial burden that buying new textbooks can place on students. Often, these books are only used for a single semester before they are left to gather dust. By providing a platform where students can sell their used textbooks to other students, we not only help reduce waste but also ensure that learning resources are accessible to everyone.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a community-driven solution for textbook exchange. Since our inception, we have grown to become an integral part of the Gitam University experience. Our user-friendly website makes it easy for students to list their books for sale, search for required textbooks, and complete transactions securely.

We pride ourselves on being more than just a bookstore; we are a community of learners supporting each other. Our team is composed of dedicated students and alumni who are passionate about education and sustainability. Together, we work tirelessly to ensure that Gitam BookStore remains a reliable and trustworthy platform for all students.

Our mission is to make education more affordable and accessible by facilitating the reuse of textbooks. By choosing to buy and sell books through Gitam BookStore, students are not only saving money but also contributing to a more sustainable and eco-friendly campus environment.

We are committed to continuously improving our services and expanding our reach within the Gitam community. If you have any suggestions or feedback, please do not hesitate to contact us. Your support and participation are what make Gitam BookStore a thriving community.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Together, we can create a more sustainable and connected campus.

Steps to Buy a Book

  1. Visit the "Buy Books" section on our website.
  2. Search for the book you need using the search bar.
  3. Browse through the list of available books and select the one you want to buy.
  4. Contact the seller using the provided details to arrange the purchase.
  5. Meet the seller in person to inspect the book and complete the transaction.
  6. Confirm the purchase on our website to complete the process.

Steps to Sell a Book

  1. Visit the "Sell Books" section on our website.
  2. Click on "Sell Now" and fill in the details of the book you want to sell.
  3. Upload clear photos of the book's cover and a few inside pages.
  4. Set a reasonable price for your book based on its condition and market value.
  5. Submit your listing and wait for interested buyers to contact you.
  6. Arrange a meeting with the buyer to show them the book and complete the sale.
  7. Confirm the sale on our website to complete the process.

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to post an ad on Clankart

Sign Up for Free

To start selling your books on Clankart, you need to first create an account on Clankart. Creating the account is very simple. All you need is:

  • Email
  • Phone Number

Listing your Books

It is very easy to upload your books on Clankart for sale! Your book ad gets live instantly after you post it.

How to Upload your Book for Sale?

Once you signup, go to the Sell Used Books page and upload your book for sale. The process to list your book is simple.

  • The first field is for the Ad Title. Enter a descriptive title for your ad that accurately represents the book you are selling.
  • The next field is Book Details. You can choose to enter the book details manually or automatically by entering the 13 digit ISBN number.
  • If you select the 'Automatically; by just typing the ISBN number' option, then all you need is to enter your book's 13 digit ISBN number. Clankart will automatically fetch all book details like the book type, book category, book title, author name, etc.
  • If the book doesn't have an ISBN, you can enter the book details manually by selecting 'Enter book details manually' option. Fill in the required fields like book type, book category, book title, author name, etc., yourself.
  • Then is Book Condition. Choose the condition that most accurately reflects the book's actual condition.
  • Next is Upload Photos. Upload at least 3 high-quality photos of the book you are selling.

Pricing Details

Set a reasonable price for your book to attract potential buyers. Specify your shipping charges upfront if you are shipping the book to the buyer. Indicate your preferred payment mode (UPI or bank account).
